¿Qué es esto?

In this page, I will try to collect all the resources that I used and that I am still using to carry out my PhD thesis. All in a single PDF file categorised by task with a handy table of contents, so you can find the tool you want as soons as you need it.

At some point of my PhD, I have used all these resources, tools, advices, applications... to make my work easier and tidier. I would recommend you to check at least the table of contents so you can become familiar with the type of tools it containts.

This work could not have been posible without the help of my friends. To all of them, thank you!

¿Por qué hago esto?

I think that sharing all the tools that helped me during my PhD thesis could be beneficial and useful for other people who are starting their thesis. Here, there are a few more reasons:

  • Getting started is difficult, and a little help always comes in handy.
  • The content of the file will be update periodically as long as I am working on my thesis.
  • It is a way to have all the tools in a single place.
  • It is a super easy way to share all this information with poeple.
  • People can send me feedback or more tools that I don't know to make it better.

I just hope that all this helps and is useful for all those who are starting their thesis or for those who are already working on it.
